I'm Emma. An aspiring multidisciplinary designer from Northern Ireland, currently studying Interaction Design full-time as an undergraduate student at the Belfast School of Art.


















A little bit about myself...

I joined Interaction Design to understand how to design websites and apps. And I hope to design a fantastic one by the end of this course at Belfast School of Art!
I feel excited to learn something that I’m passionate about - my childhood revolved around technology, so it feels incredible to understand what my heroes used to create their successful businesses.
I first got introduced to technology as a child from my first ever gaming console – a Nintendo DS Lite. And this is what led me to fall in love with art and technology.
I hope to become an independent multidisciplinary designer for the digitally influenced future, combining knowledge from both a logical and creative-thinking perspective to tackle today’s complex problems.
